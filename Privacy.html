Your Privacy Matters

At Thanks A Day, we respect and prioritize your privacy. This privacy policy outlines how we handle and protect your data.

What Data We Collect

  • Gratitude Entries: Your entries are private by default and stored securely. They are only shared if you choose to.
  • Images: Any images you attach to your entries are stored securely and are private by default.
  • Email Addresses: We collect your email address when you sign up or log in to Thanks A Day. This is used exclusively for login purposes.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data primarily to provide the core features of Thanks A Day. Specifically, your email address is used for secure login to your account. We do not sell, lease, or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes.

Storage of Data

Your data, including your email address, is stored securely on Firebase, a database managed by Google, and hosted in Europe.

Access to Your Data

You can access your stored data, including your email address, at any time by logging into your account. If you wish to modify or delete this information, please contact us at fgouin.a2z@gmail.com.

Security

We take all necessary security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, modification, disclosure, or destruction.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page, so please review it periodically. If significant changes are made, we may notify you by email.
