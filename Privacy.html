Introduction

Thank you for using Thank You Today. This Privacy Policy applies to all data collected by A2ZFG Corp. through the Thank You Today application.

Data Collection

  • Authentication: We use your email address for authentication, which is managed by Firebase. We recommend using "Apple Sign In" as it allows you not to share your email address.
  • Statistics and Analytics: We use Google Analytics to collect data on how you use our application.
  • Advertisements: We display non-personalized advertisements in our application.
  • Subscription: We collect data related to your monthly or yearly subscription for premium features.

Subscription Details

We offer monthly and yearly subscriptions that unlock premium features and remove advertisements. Your subscription will automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

Renewal Policy

Subscriptions are set to automatically renew, and you will be billed in advance within 24 hours of the end of the current subscription period. You can cancel your subscription at any time by going to your App Store account settings.

Payment Processing

All payment transactions are processed securely through the App Store. We do not handle or store any financial information.

Refund Policy

Refund requests must be made through the App Store. We do not process refunds directly.

Data Usage

Subscription data is used exclusively to unlock premium features and remove advertisements. No additional personal data is collected as part of the subscription service.

Opt-Out

You can manage your subscription or opt-out of auto-renewal through your App Store account settings.

Data Usage

Your data may be used to provide technical support and for statistical purposes, without ever using or disclosing your identity.

Data Sharing

We share data only with Google for statistical and analytics services.

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. All data are encrypted and stored in servers located in Europe.

Your Rights

You have the right to review, modify, or delete your personal data at any time.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy. Changes will take effect immediately upon being posted on this page.

Contact Us
