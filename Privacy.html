Your Privacy Matters

At Thanks A Day, we respect and prioritize your privacy. This privacy policy outlines how we handle and protect your data.

What Data We Collect

  • Gratitude Entries: Your entries are private by default and stored securely. They are only shared if you choose to.
  • Images: Any images you attach to your entries are stored securely and are private by default.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data primarily to provide the core features of Thanks A Day. We do not sell, lease, or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page, so please review it periodically.
