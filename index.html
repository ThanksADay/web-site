About Thanks A Day

Thanks A Day is an innovative app that encourages gratitude in everyday life. Through simple and engaging features, users can capture moments of gratitude and share their positive vibes with the world.

Features

  • No Ads: Enjoy an uninterrupted experience without any ads.
  • Manage Categories: Customize and organize your gratitude moments in categories that resonate with you.
  • Speech to Text: Speak your heart out and let our app convert it into text for you.
  • Custom Share: Share your gratitude moments in a personalized way.
  • Subscription Plans: Choose between monthly and yearly subscription plans for enhanced features.
